Campus Features

Built for Real-World
Mastery

Both campuses feature purpose-built environments that mirror actual industry conditions — so every student trains on what they'll face in the field.

  • Industry-Standard Tools

    Snap-on, Bosch, and Autel diagnostic equipment — the same brands you'll find at dealerships.

  • Real Vehicle Fleet

    Passenger cars, trucks, and hybrid models available for every practical session.

  • Dedicated ARPL Assessment Rooms

    Private, purpose-designed bays for formal recognition of prior learning assessments.

  • THRASS® Educator Resource Centre

    A full literacy training library with printed and digital THRASS® educator materials.
