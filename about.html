Rooted in Excellence,
Forged for the Future

Established in June 2023 following the strategic acquisition of the prestigious Bidvest McCarthy Automotive Training Academy, Next Level Skills Academy carries forward a rich legacy spanning over a decade of artisan development.

With 5,000+ artisans trained and dual operations in Gauteng (Midrand) and KwaZulu-Natal (Pinetown), we've become the benchmark for technical excellence. We bridge the gap between theory and real-world workshop mastery — offering accredited trade tests, ARPL assessments, and Red Seal qualifications.
