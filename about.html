Why We Exist

Our Mission
& Vision

To eradicate illiteracy across Africa by providing world-class, evidence-based phonics resources that make learning to read accessible, joyful, and effective for every child — regardless of background or first language.

We believe literacy is the foundation of all learning. Through the THRASS methodology — a systematic, multi-sensory approach to phonics — we equip educators and parents with tools that unlock reading fluency from the very first sounds.

Our Vision
A continent where every child reads with confidence — from Cape Town to Cairo, one sound at a time.
Core Principles

Milestones

The Journey So Far

A decade of dedication, growth, and impact across Southern Africa.

2015

Foundation of Next Level Learning

Started with a bold mission to bring THRASS methodology to South African schools — partnering with literacy experts, educators, and curriculum specialists to build something lasting.

2018

First Teacher Training Cohort

Trained over 200 educators in Gauteng and Western Cape, witnessing literacy gains of up to 40% in participating schools within a single academic year.

2021

Digital Expansion & New Resources

Launched interactive digital workbooks, phonics chart series, and decodable reader sets — reaching 150+ schools across 5 provinces with both print and digital solutions.

2024 – 2026

National Recognition & Growth

Recognised as a premier THRASS partner, now serving 350+ schools and 24,000+ learners with the continent's most comprehensive phonics literacy solution.
