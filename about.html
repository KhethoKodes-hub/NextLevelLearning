Our Heritage

Rooted in Excellence,
Forged for the Future

Established in June 2023 following the strategic acquisition of the prestigious Bidvest McCarthy Automotive Training Academy, Next Level Skills Academy carries forward a rich legacy of artisan development. With over a decade of combined industry experience and more than 5,000 artisans trained, we have expanded from a single location to dual operations in Gauteng (Midrand) and KwaZulu-Natal (Pinetown).

We don't just train — we mentor. Our purpose is to bridge the gap between theory and real-world workshop excellence while offering accredited trade tests and ARPL assessments that open doors to Red Seal qualifications.

THRASS® Methodology:
Teaching Handwriting, Reading & Spelling Skills

At Next Level Skills Academy, our expertise extends to empowering educators. We publish specialized training materials based on the internationally recognized THRASS® (Teaching Handwriting, Reading And Spelling Skills) methodology — a phonetic, multi-sensory approach that builds literacy foundations. Through workshops, resources, and teacher training, we help schools and education professionals unlock the potential of every learner. Just as we build world-class mechanics, we build world-class teachers who shape future generations.
