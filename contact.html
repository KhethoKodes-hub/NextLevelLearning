Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out — straight answers, no jargon.

How do I order THRASS resources?

Order via our contact form, email us directly, or call our office. We offer bulk discounts for schools purchasing multiple sets.

Do you offer teacher training?

Yes — in-person and virtual THRASS teacher training available. Contact us to schedule a session for your school, district, or department.

What's the order turnaround time?

Orders are processed within 2–3 business days. Delivery within South Africa typically takes 3–7 working days depending on location.

Can we become a school partner?

Absolutely! Our school partnership programme offers preferential pricing, dedicated support, and ongoing professional development for your staff.

Are digital resources available?

Yes — interactive digital phonics charts, e-workbooks, and online teacher training modules are available alongside our print materials.

Can I get a free consultation?

Of course! Our literacy specialists offer complimentary consultations to assess your school's needs. Fill in the form above and we'll schedule a call.
