Official accredited testing and recognition of prior learning — your gateway to formal artisan qualification.

Trade Test Centre

merSETA Accredited · QCTO Aligned

We are an official accredited Trade Test Centre (TTC) for Motor Mechanic, Diesel Mechanic, and Automotive Electrician trades. Our assessments meet the highest national standards for fairness and credibility.

  • Legacy Trade Level Testing

    Structured progression with official assessment at each level.

  • Final Red Seal Trade Tests

    The nationally recognised gold-standard artisan qualification.

  • Fair & Transparent Assessment

    Independent assessors, standardised criteria, full candidate feedback.

  • Flexible Scheduling

    Tests conducted at both our Midrand and Pinetown campuses.

ARPL Services

Recognition of Prior Learning

The Artisan Recognition of Prior Learning pathway opens doors for experienced tradespeople without formal apprenticeships. We provide end-to-end guidance — from initial assessment to Red Seal certification.

  • Eligibility Assessment

    We evaluate your experience and determine your ARPL readiness.

  • Portfolio of Evidence

    We guide you through documenting skills, work history, and competencies.

  • Bridging Programme

    Targeted upskilling to close any gaps before formal assessment.

  • Trade Test & Certification

    Sit your Red Seal test and receive your formal artisan qualification.
