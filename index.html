Trade Test Centre

Your Gateway to
Artisan Qualification

We are an accredited TTC conducting level testing, final Red Seal trade tests, and formal ARPL assessments with fairness and full transparency.

  • Legacy Trade Level Testing

    Structured progression through trade levels with official assessment at each stage.

  • Final Red Seal Trade Tests

    The nationally recognised qualification — the gold standard for South African artisans.

  • ARPL Pathway

    Recognition of Prior Learning for experienced candidates without formal apprenticeship.

  • Fair & Transparent Assessment

ARPL Services

Experience Is Your
Qualification

No formal apprenticeship? No problem. The Artisan Recognition of Prior Learning pathway allows experienced tradespeople to earn formal qualifications.

1

Initial Consultation & Application

We assess your experience and determine eligibility for the ARPL pathway.

2

Evidence Portfolio Compilation

We guide you through documenting your skills, experience, and competencies.

3

Bridging Programme (if required)

Targeted upskilling to close any gaps before the formal assessment.

4

Trade Test Admission & Certification

Sit your Red Seal trade test and receive your formal artisan qualification.
