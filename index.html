Next Level Learning is a pioneering South African educational publisher dedicated to transforming literacy through the evidence-based THRASS (Teaching Handwriting Reading And Spelling Skills) programme — the gold standard in systematic phonics instruction.

We equip teachers, parents, and learners with structured, research-driven resources that unlock reading fluency from the very first sounds. Our mission: "No child left behind in literacy."

The Journey

How THRASS Works

1

Sound Recognition

Children learn to identify and categorise the 44 speech sounds of the English language through systematic, multisensory activities.

2

Grapheme Mapping

Students discover how sounds connect to written letters and letter combinations — building a complete, logical map of English spelling.

3

Fluent Reading

Armed with phonics knowledge, learners decode any word confidently and develop genuine reading fluency and a love for books.
